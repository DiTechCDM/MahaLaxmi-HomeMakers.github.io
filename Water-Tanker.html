About Our Water Transportation

Our Transportation began as a platform to improve the accessibility of water to people across the country. Many areas across India lack basic supply infrastructure like piped water. Most cities even with this infrastructure fails to supply fresh water due to acute shortage. Water tankers are operated across the country to supply groundwater to the people in need. However, this has led to over exploitation of groundwater table in many regions.
