Evolution

An Overview

With Mumbai and Thane reaching saturation due to paucity of land parcels and soaring property prices, newer areas have emerged on the peripheries.

Strategically located between Mira-Bhayandar and Vasai-Virar, Naigaon is one such micromarket that offers a massive end-use and investment opportunity for homebuyers and investors alike

From a quaint suburb with pristine beaches to an emerging real estate hotspot, the metamorphosis of Naigaon is nothing short of remarkable
