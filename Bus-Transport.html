About MBMT

MBMT (Mira Bhayandar Municipal Transport) also known as MBMT, is a public transportation provider in Mumbai and Thane that operates Bus routes from 2005-to 2010.

The MBMT (Mira Bhayandar Municipal Transport) has 60-70 Bus routes in Mumbai with 700- 800 Bus stops.

And based on the New tender we have the project ongoing from 2020, we have 65 Buses on street every day as a part of our ongoing project to different routes and except on holidays we have reduced the flow of buses .

Coverage Area

The MBMT (Mira Bhayandar Municipal Transport) has Bus routes operating across Mumbai including Mira Bhayandar, and Borivali, Andheri and Thane

According to Moovit Insights, the most popular lines for MBMT (Mira Bhayandar Municipal Transport) are 10 & 7A.
